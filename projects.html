Commercial Projects


These projects span various aspects of commercial construction, including retail buildings, fuel distribution infrastructure, office buildings, and convenience facilities. They demonstrate a history of development and expansion within the fuel and retail sectors, with a focus on providing services and amenities to customers.

High-Quality Interior Fit-Out Projects


We take pride in our completed construction projects, particularly in the realm of interior fit-outs. Among our achievements, we've successfully executed contracts for ten Foody's stores within the Shell Retail Station Network from 2015 to 2023. These projects showcase our commitment to delivering excellence in interior design and construction, ensuring both functionality and aesthetics are seamlessly integrated.

Federal Projects


Our involvement in federal projects spans diverse endeavors. Presently, we're engaged in the reconstruction and widening of Rte 14B, Phase 1, with completion projected for June 2024. Additionally, we've subcontracted several roof repairs with waterproofing at Andersen Air Force Base (AAFB) and US Navy installations, showcasing our capability to contribute effectively to large-scale infrastructure initiatives.

Maintenance Services


Our maintenance division caters to a diverse clientele, providing essential services for both Shell Retail Stations and commercial clients. Our comprehensive maintenance solutions encompass routine servicing, repairs, and upkeep to ensure optimal functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Additionally, we extend our maintenance expertise to serve the OLOP Cemetery, where we undertake a range of maintenance and repair tasks. Through our diligent efforts, we strive to uphold the sanctity and pristine condition of this significant site.
