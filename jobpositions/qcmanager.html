At J&L Builders Corporation, we believe in the power of talent, innovation, and collaboration to drive success. Our commitment to excellence extends not only to the products and services we offer but also to the talented individuals who make up our team.

QC Manager

Job Summary:

The Quality Control (QC) Manager plays a pivotal role in ensuring the adherence to quality standards and procedures within our organization. This position involves overseeing the implementation of quality control processes across various projects and departments, aiming to deliver products and services that meet or exceed customer expectations.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Develop and implement quality control policies and procedures to ensure compliance with regulatory standards and customer requirements.
  • Conduct regular inspections and audits to assess the quality of products, processes, and services, identifying areas for improvement.
  • Collaborate with project managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to address quality issues and implement corrective actions.
  • Establish quality metrics and performance indicators to monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of quality control processes.
  • Provide training and guidance to staff on quality control methods, procedures, and best practices.
  • Investigate customer complaints and non-conformance issues, determining root causes and implementing preventative measures.
  • Maintain documentation and records related to quality control activities, ensuring accuracy and accessibility for audits and reviews.
  • Stay abreast of industry trends, developments, and regulatory changes related to quality control practices.
  • Foster a culture of quality consciousness and continuous improvement throughout the organization.
Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify and resolve quality issues effectively.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Detail-oriented and organized, with a commitment to accuracy and precision in all work activities.
  • Proficiency in quality control tools and techniques, including statistical analysis and root cause analysis.
  • Familiarity with relevant quality management systems and standards, such as ISO 9001.
  • Knowledge of industry-specific quality requirements and regulations.
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and manage time effectively in a fast-paced environment.
Education and Experience:
  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field, such as engineering, quality management, or a related discipline.
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in quality control or a related field.
  • Certification in quality management or related areas (e.g., Six Sigma, Lean, ASQ) is a plus.

If you meet the qualifications and are passionate about ensuring quality excellence, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity to join our team as a QC Manager.
