ABOUT US


Established in 2012, J&L Builders has become a leading small business Corporation dedicated to providing top-notch construction services. Specializing in residential, commercial, and Federal projects, we've consistently delivered successful results, with a notable $1,086,000.00 gross contract achieved in 2023. With a target project worth of $2,000,000.00 this year, our growth trajectory is evident.


Offering general construction, Federal projects, and maintenance services, we pride ourselves on a diverse portfolio. From commercial ventures like Shell Retail Building/Foodys & Carwash construction to high-quality interior fit-outs, our expertise spans various sectors. With a skilled team of twenty craftsmen, J&L Builders ensures superior project delivery meeting the highest standards, delivered promptly with exceptional service.
